What is Astronomy?

Astronomy is branch of science which focuses on celestial objects and the night sky. The experts in astronomy are called astronomers and they use tools such as telescopes, satelites and computers to map and observe the body that we call the physical universe. However, humans are only justs scratching the surface of astronomy and there is lots more to discover in the emptiness of space.

Common Astronomical Terms

  • Celestial Objects

  • A celestial object is a term used to describe an object in space. It does not have a specific size, mass or gravitational force
